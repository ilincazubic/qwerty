Introduction

Hello. My name is Ilinca and I am a 16-year-old teenager. I always liked to cook various sweets, biscuits, cakes, and since I was little I dreamed of opening my own bakery shop when I grow up that would produce the most delicious and special products and be known all over the world. I grew up and this dream came true. At only 16 years old, I opened my own bakery shop, of course with the help of family, friends, with a lot of work, patience and self-confidence. My next goal is to open more bakeries in different countries and sweeten people's lives with my delicious products. Until then, I present you my bakery shop:

SWEET TIME BAKERY

Information

Our bakers start work before the sun rises each day to ensure that every batch is as fresh as possible before it gets to you. The warm smell of baking often escapes onto the street before we've even opened our doors. We're dedicated to kick starting your day with fresh bread, donuts, brownies, pudding, sweets, cupcakes, mini cakes, tea cakes, fresh fruits, delicious yummy ice cream, and so much more.
